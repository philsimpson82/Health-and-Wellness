Massage and health benfits

Often massage is seen as just for relaxation, which it is that, but there are many other benefits that come with regular massage therapy work. Some of these benefits are stress relief by lowering the cortisol horomone, improvement of circulation, increased mobility and range of motion, reduction in chronic pain, and soft tissue recovery after work and exercise.

About the Owner

Andrea has been practicing massage therapy since she received her certificat from Heritage College in 2010. It has always been a passion of hers to serve others. Massage therapy helps increase overall health and decrease pain, and her specialties are deep tissue, trigger point therapy, prenatal massage, and relaxing Swedish as well.
Along with running Rest & Wellness Massage, Andrea enjoys learning more about how to build health, travel with her husband, and play with all of her nieces and nephews. Life is good and getting to help others through massage therapy the last 12 years is her favorite thing.
